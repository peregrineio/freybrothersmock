Our Story

Frey Brothers Electricians isn't a franchise. It isn't a call center. When you call us, you get one of us, the actual Frey brothers who will be working in your home.

We grew up in Texas, learning the trade hands-on. We started this business because we believe electrical work should be done right, priced fairly, and finished on time.

No corporate overhead. No sales pressure. No strangers in your house. Just two brothers who take pride in their work and treat every customer like a neighbor, because that's exactly what you are.

When you hire Frey Brothers, you're hiring family.
