Panel Services

▼

Older homes often have 100-amp or even 60-amp panels that can't handle modern electrical loads. New panels increase capacity, improve safety, and are often required for EV chargers or home additions.

  • Electrical panel upgrades
  • Circuit breaker replacement
  • Subpanel installation
  • Load calculations
  • Safety inspections
🌀

Ceiling Fans

▼

Ceiling fans involve both electrical wiring and mounting to ceiling joists. Improper installation can cause wobbling, noise, or fire hazards. Let a pro handle it.

  • Ceiling fan installation
  • Ceiling fan replacement
  • Fan repair & troubleshooting
  • Balancing & noise fixes
