Home/Service Areas/Brenham

Brenham's Trusted Electricians for Heritage Homes, Ranches, and Local Business

Brenham is one of those rare Texas towns that has held on to its character while still moving forward. The rolling hills of Washington County, the wildflower trails every spring, the antique shops along downtown, the steady stream of visitors heading to Blue Bell Creamery or Washington-on-the-Brazos State Historic Site. There is a pride here that runs deep, and Frey Brothers Electricians is honored to serve this community.

Many homes in Brenham carry real history. Victorian-era houses, early 1900s farmsteads, and mid-century ranch homes all have their own electrical quirks. Outdated wiring, overloaded panels, and fixtures that have not been touched in generations are common. We specialize in carefully rewiring and upgrading these older properties, preserving their charm while making them safe and functional for modern life.

Brenham is also a destination. Bed-and-breakfasts, event venues, and tourism-facing businesses need reliable electrical systems that can handle guest loads, commercial kitchens, landscape lighting, and signage. We work with property owners to make sure their electrical systems are up to the task, whether that means upgrading a panel for a B&B or wiring a new barn venue outside of town.

For the ranches and estates in the surrounding countryside, we handle large-property electrical needs including security lighting, outbuilding wiring, well pump circuits, and generator installations.

From a historic home on the square to a working ranch off FM 577, call us for a free estimate on any electrical project in Brenham.
