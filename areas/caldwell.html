Electricians Who Understand Country Living

Caldwell is the kind of town where a handshake still means something. As the seat of Burleson County and home to the famous Kolache Festival, this community runs on hard work, Czech heritage, and looking out for your neighbors. We respect that, and we bring the same values to every job we do here.

Whether you need wiring for a new barn, a panel upgrade on an older county home, or circuits added for equipment in an agricultural outbuilding, Frey Brothers Electricians understands the unique demands of rural property electrical work. We know that out here, you need an electrician who shows up when they say they will, does clean work, and charges what the job is actually worth. No city markups, no surprise fees.

Call us for a free estimate. We're happy to drive out, walk your property, and give you an honest quote.
