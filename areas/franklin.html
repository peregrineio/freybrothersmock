A Family Business for a Family Town

Franklin is the kind of place where everyone at the feed store knows your name, where the courthouse square still feels like the center of town, and where families have worked the same land for generations. It is quiet, self-reliant, and proud. We understand that, because we are a family business too.

Out here, electrical work often means more than just a house. It means wiring a workshop so you can keep your equipment running, adding circuits to a barn, installing a well pump on a back pasture, or getting a whole-home generator so your family is not left in the dark when a storm rolls through Robertson County. Frey Brothers Electricians handles all of it. We drive out to your property, look at the job with our own eyes, and give you an honest number. No pressure, no games, and no extra charge just because you live outside city limits.

From the courthouse square to the county line, call us for a free estimate on any electrical work in Franklin.
