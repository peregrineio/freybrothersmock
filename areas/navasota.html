Down-to-Earth Electricians for Navasota and Grimes County

Navasota is the kind of place where people still wave from their trucks and know their neighbors by name. It is also the kind of place where properties come in all shapes and sizes. You might have a house in town that was built in the 1950s and still has its original panel. Or you might be on ten acres outside of town with a barn, a workshop, and an outbuilding that all need power run to them. Either way, Frey Brothers Electricians is the crew to call.

Living in Grimes County means dealing with Texas weather. Summer storms and power outages are a fact of life out here. We install whole-home generators and transfer switches so your family is never left in the dark. We also set up surge protection systems to keep your appliances and electronics safe when the power comes back on and spikes.

For folks with larger rural properties, we install security lighting, wire workshops and barns for tools and equipment, and run dedicated circuits for welders, compressors, and other heavy-draw machinery. We understand that out here, your electrical system is not just about comfort. It is about keeping your property and livelihood running.

Whether you are in town or twenty minutes down a county road, give us a call for a free estimate. We will come to you.
