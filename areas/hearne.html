Wiring Hearne for the Next Generation

Hearne was built at the crossroads of Texas railroads, and that working-class grit still runs through the community today. From the neighborhoods near the Hearne Municipal Airport area to the farming properties stretching toward Mumford and Benchley, this town has deep roots in Robertson County. Many of the homes here go back decades. Some were built when the railroad was still king. The wiring in those houses tells the story of a different era, and it often needs updating to keep families safe.

Frey Brothers Electricians specializes in the kind of work Hearne needs most. We bring older homes up to modern code, handle mobile home electrical upgrades safely and properly, and take care of commercial electrical for local businesses along Highway 6. We are not a big-city company sending crews from far away. We are a family operation, and we treat every job in Hearne like we are working on our own house.

Whether it is a 1950s bungalow or a brand-new build, give us a call for a free estimate.
