Electricians Who Know College Station Inside and Out

College Station runs on energy. Between Texas A&M students moving into rental houses every August, new subdivisions going up south of Rock Prairie, and homeowners upgrading panels to keep up with modern demand, there is always electrical work to be done here. Frey Brothers Electricians has been part of this community from the start, and we understand what Aggieland needs.

We work on everything from older homes near campus with outdated wiring to brand-new builds in Castlegate and Creek Meadows. If you are a landlord prepping a rental property for the next round of tenants, we can get your outlets, breakers, and fixtures inspection-ready fast. If you just bought your first home and want an EV charger in the garage or better outdoor lighting for hosting on game day weekends, we handle that too.

This is a town that moves fast and expects things done right. That is exactly how we work. No runaround, no upselling. Just honest electrical service from a family-owned crew that takes pride in the Brazos Valley.

Whether it is a quick repair or a full rewire, call us for a free estimate on any electrical work in College Station.
