Bryan's Electricians for Historic Homes and Growing Neighborhoods

Bryan has a character all its own. The historic district is full of beautiful older homes with original woodwork, high ceilings, and, in many cases, electrical systems that have not been touched in decades. Knob-and-tube wiring, undersized panels, and aluminum branch circuits are things we see regularly in this part of town. Frey Brothers Electricians specializes in bringing these homes into the modern era safely, without cutting corners or cutting into your walls more than necessary.

But Bryan is not just its history. Downtown Bryan has seen an incredible revival over the past several years. New restaurants, galleries, and small businesses are opening up along Main Street and beyond. We provide commercial electrical services for these growing businesses, from lighting design for storefronts to dedicated circuits for kitchen equipment to full tenant build-outs.

And for the families moving into newer neighborhoods on Bryan's east and north sides, we handle panel upgrades, ceiling fan installs, outdoor lighting, and everything else that comes with settling into a home and making it yours.

From a 1920s bungalow in the historic district to a new build off Villa Maria, call us for a free estimate on any electrical project in Bryan.
